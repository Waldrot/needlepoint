Victor Villavicencio

Grafisk formgivare och Illustratör.
Medlem av Svenska Tecknare.


Certified as a multimedia producer already in 1997 at the Capital School of Multimedia. Studies in graphic design at Forsberg School. After taking a bachelor's degree in media technology at Södertörn University, he was handpicked for the EU project Immigrant Inclusion by eParticipation (IIeP), where he researched social media as a tool for integration and inclusion.
He has previously worked as a web designer and final art, created illustrations and technical descriptions for companies in the process industry and was a network manager. Came from Chile as a little boy and still dislikes Kalle's caviar. Victor does illustrations for Swedavia and Wasa Kredit, among others.
